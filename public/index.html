DANIELE TENGA IS A DEVELOPER AND DESIGNER

Daniele Tenga is a developer based in Atlanta, Ga. She uses human-centered design to create a digital experience and services that are intuitive and accessible. She is currently working to enhance the User experience at Vision13.

Welcome to my portfolio. This webpage is a snippet of my vibrant personality and all the things that make me who I am. Recent Georgia State graduate with an anthropology degree I felt like something was missing. I wanted to do something that would keep me constantly challenged so I decided to pursue my masters in information technology. One of the requirements for the application was to create a code. And that's when I discovered a developer's world and began investing my time in learning HTML, CSS, Python and Javascript. I am self taught and open to constanlty learning. Life is nothing without challenges.Im an architect at heart. I love to design and build and push the boundaries of what it means to be in a box. I enjoyed creating all of these projects. The home page consists of a collage of pictures from different aspects of my creative mind; What motivates me, what inspires me, what keeps me going. Although I am not a mother, motherhood is what inspires me to keep pushing because I want to be an inspiration to my future kids and lead by example. Every picture is dear to my heart and shows a little glimpse of my character. My about page as you could already tell is a little bit about me. My projects page is a composition of all the projects that I have created, including this webpage. I fought with every ounce of me with everything I created on this page. I missed meals, sometimes I wanted to throw my computer. But there is just something about having a vision that you just have to see come to life. There are a lot of things I have explored during this time. Each picture that is included in my collage is a part of me that is very dear to my heart but also an explanation of my charater. And although I am not a mother, motherhood is a huge motivation for me because I want to be an example to my future kids. I want to lead by example.
And that..... That, is what fuels my tenacity. I am so excited for what you all are about to discover. Thank you for taking the time out to check out my work.
